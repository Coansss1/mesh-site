how we mesh

from first call to final report.

I.

Discovery & Strategy

We open with objectives: what are you trying to achieve, what are your KPIs, what are the non-negotiables. We define goals, audience, tone, and format, and we challenge assumptions: is the agenda too heavy? Is there enough breathing room? Most companies overpack their schedules and don't think about how people feel by day three. We do.

II.

Planning & Partnerships

Destination consulting based on budget, season, and team distribution. Venue scouting in person, always: multiple options presented, physical visits and menu tastings before committing. Suppliers vetted face to face. Contracts, budgets, payments, and compliance overseen at every step.

III.

Production & On-Site Management

From setup to strike: staging, sound, AV, signage, decoration, photography, podcast setups, and internet infrastructure (we bring our own Starlink when venues fall short). Someone physically waiting at airport arrivals. Both founders present, running the full show.

IV.

Close-Out & Reporting

Billing finalized, KPI reports delivered, debriefs conducted. Every event makes the next one better, because the relationship deepens: the second event is better than the first, the third better than the second. We already know your people, your culture, and your standards.

planning ahead

the best venues go first.

The ideal engagement starts 6 to 12 months out. That gives us access to the best venues and suppliers, with 80 to 90% of production locked months before the event. The remaining time goes into refinements and the extras that turn a good event into an exceptional one.

  • 12+ months out: ideal. Best venues, best prices, maximum creativity
  • 6 to 12 months: great. Still plenty of options
  • 3 to 6 months: doable, but some options will be gone

Already have an events person? Even better. We're not a replacement, we're the production engine behind your internal point of contact.

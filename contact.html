what the first call looks like

we start with your objectives, not our pitch.

What are you trying to achieve with this event? What does success look like? Then the practical frame: team size, preferred dates, destination ideas, budget range, non-negotiables. For retreats: how work-heavy versus leisure, what kinds of activities fit your team, dietary needs.

And we do our own homework. Before the first meeting ends, we'll already know your company, your founders' background, and your business context.

  • Timing: the ideal start is 6 to 12 months out. The best venues get booked fast.
  • Scale: from 40-person leadership retreats to 800-person conferences.
  • Geography: rooted in Portugal, producing worldwide. 13 countries and counting.
  • You have an events person? Perfect. We're the production engine behind them, not a replacement.
